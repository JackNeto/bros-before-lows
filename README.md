# Bros Before Lows

A men's mental health initiative website for Bros Before Lows, a Brampton-based organization dedicated to reducing male suicide rates and providing accessible peer support.

## About

Bros Before Lows is a movement for men's mental health grounded in healing, brotherhood, and purpose. The organization emphasizes the power of positive male connection during life's toughest battles — whether mental health struggles, substance abuse, or unhealthy relationships.
