What is Bros Before Lows?

Bros Before Lows isn't just a slogan — it's a movement.

A Movement for Men's Mental Health

It is a movement for men's mental health grounded in healing, brotherhood and purpose.

We're here to emphasize the power of positive male connection — especially during life's toughest battles, whether it's mental health struggles, substance abuse, or unhealthy relationships.

Upcoming Event

Join us for a powerful night blending music with mental health advocacy.

Saturday, August 30, 2025

Talking Saves Lives

Venue Spot 1 Bar & Grill, 289 Rutherford Rd S, Brampton, ON Goal Raise $6,000 for men's peer-support work Featuring Special guest Atif Rashid, Brampton's own hip-hop artist & influencer Spitty, and local openers

We're aiming to raise $6,000 to power our men's peer-support work — and we're doing it the best way we know how: by blending music with mental-health advocacy.

Our Mission

Building a world where every man feels seen, heard, and valued.

Create safe, inclusive spaces where men of all ages can express themselves openly and without judgment

Blend music with mental health advocacy as a powerful tool for emotional expression, healing, and connection

Build a peer-led support network where every man feels seen, heard, and valued

Break the stigma surrounding men's mental health by encouraging real, vulnerable conversations

Promote a partnership-based approach to healing — brothers helping brothers

"If you're ready to fight for your life, we're ready to fight beside you — and make sure you never have to fight alone."

Our Current Focus

We're building this from the ground up — and with your support, we're ready to move fast and show up where we're needed most.

Expand Peer-Led Support Groups

Across the GTA and beyond — making mental health support more accessible to men in every stage of life.

Integrate Music Therapy Tools

Into gatherings and events, using instruments, songwriting, and live performance as forms of emotional release.

Launch the BBL Foundation

Fundraising for the official launch of The Bros Before Lows Foundation — so we can build long-term impact, faster.

Develop Sustainable Outreach

To connect with young men, fathers, and those in recovery, offering creative, community-driven resources.

Join the Waiting List

Be the first to know when our Peer Support Groups launch in your area.

Bros Before Lows is a Brampton-based men's mental health initiative dedicated to reducing male suicide rates and providing accessible peer support.
