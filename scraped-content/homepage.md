## Core Mission Statement
Bros Before Lows is "a movement for men's mental health grounded in healing, brotherhood and purpose."

Key focuses include:
- Creating safe spaces for vulnerability
- Community-driven connections
- Environments where men navigate challenges with support rather than silence
