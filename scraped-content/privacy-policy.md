# Bros Before Lows - Privacy Policy

## Data Collection

### Personal Information Provided
BBL collects:
- Account details
- Purchase information
- Payment data
- Transaction history
- Communications

As stated: "When you create an account with us, we will collect information associated with your account, including...your name, contact information, account credentials, payment card information."

### Automatically Collected Data
The site gathers:
- Browsing behavior
- Device identifiers
- IP addresses
- Location inferences

They note: "We may collect information about the way you use the Services, such as the frequency of your use, the types of content that you view or engage with."

## Cookies & Tracking
BBL uses cookies, pixels, and analytics tools to monitor user activity.

The policy states: "We and those of our vendors that provide us online analytics products may use cookies, clear GIFs, pixel tags, and other tracking technologies."

- Users can manage consent preferences on the website
- Disabling cookies may limit service functionality

## Data Usage
Information supports:
- Service delivery
- Fraud prevention
- Marketing
- Legal compliance
- Business analytics

They use data "To provide, maintain, improve, and enhance the Services" and for "marketing purposes."

## User Rights
Depending on location, users may:
- Access their data
- Correct their data
- Delete their data
- Transfer their data

### California Residents (CCPA)
Additional rights including opt-outs for "sales" or "sharing" of personal information.

The policy advises: "To opt out of our disclosure of your personal information...please send the request via email."
