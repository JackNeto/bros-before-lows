# Bros Before Lows - Returns & FAQ

## Quality Guarantee
BBL replaces or refunds items with print errors or visible quality issues upon provision of clear photos on a flat, well-lit surface submitted via their contact form.

## Return Policy
**No General Returns:** "Because products are made to order, we do not accept general returns or sizing-related returns."

## Cancellations

### Product Orders
- Customers may edit orders anytime before production begins using a link in the confirmation email
- After production starts, replacements/resolutions depend on the situation

### Donations & Tips
"Non-cancellable and non-refundable."

### Locked Messages
"Non-refundable. Once you have completed your checkout payment, we cannot cancel or refund your locked message."

- Customers are responsible for import taxes and duties
- These vary by country and cannot be calculated in advance

## Quality Issue Resolution
For fastest resolution, provide:
1. Order number
2. Photos showing the tag
3. Photos showing the error clearly on a flat surface
